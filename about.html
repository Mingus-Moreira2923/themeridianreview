About Us

Our Mission

The mission of STEM for Econ is to integrate the power of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics into the study and application of economics, equipping future leaders with the analytical tools and interdisciplinary knowledge necessary to address the complex challenges of a global economy. STEM for Econ is committed to advancing research, education, and practical innovation at the intersection of STEM and economic theory. Through collaboration, experimentation, and inquiry, we aim to foster a new generation of economists who are not only fluent in quantitative methods and technological advances, but also driven to apply these skills toward sustainable growth, equitable policy, and economic resilience. We strive to cultivate a dynamic, inclusive environment where curiosity meets impact, and where knowledge serves as a catalyst for human progress.

Our Vision

Our vision at STEM for Econ is to redefine the future of economics through the seamless integration of STEM disciplines, shaping a world where data-driven insights, technological innovation, and scientific rigor inform economic understanding and decision-making. We envision a global community of economists, scientists, and engineers working together to solve pressing economic challenges—from poverty and inequality to automation and climate change. By bridging these fields, we aim to inspire transformative solutions, foster inclusive prosperity, and build an economy that is intelligent, adaptive, and just. Our overarching goal is a world where poverty is exterminated, and humanity can reach new front tears
